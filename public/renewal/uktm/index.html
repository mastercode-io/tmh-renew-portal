A Trademark Audit goes beyond a basic search. It’s a full review of potential risks and conflicts that could threaten your brand, now or in the future.

Our Audit can include some or all the following:

Registers

Worldwide trademark registers check.

Search engines

Major search engines for confusingly similar uses.

Social & e‑commerce

Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, Amazon, eBay and more.

Domains

Conflicting domain registrations.

Companies House

Company name conflicts and risks.

Practical actions

Clear steps to strengthen protection.

Using industry-standard systems, we gather and analyse this data to give you a clear picture of potential risks, plus practical steps to strengthen your protection.

Why combine Audit, Renewal and Monitoring?

Your renewal is the perfect moment to check whether your trademark still reflects your business and remains fully protected. Combining all three services at renewal saves time, prevents confusion, ensures ongoing protection against infringers and impersonators, and saves money.
